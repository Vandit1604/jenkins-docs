Joining the Community

Have a good idea for how Blue Ocean can be improved? There a few ways you can join the community and contribute to the project:

  • Chat with the community team on Gitter.

  • Request features or report bugs against the blueocean-plugin component in JIRA.

  • Subscribe and ask questions on the Jenkins Users mailing list.

  • Developer? We’ve labeled a few issues that are great for anyone wanting to get started developing Blue Ocean. Don’t forget to drop by the Gitter chat and introduce yourself!
